Computer Science Learning Roadmap

A structured progression through key topics in Computer Science, including extensive practical projects, to prepare for Software Engineering roles.

Tips for Your Learning Journey:

  • Build a strong foundation in each level before moving on.
  • **Crucially, apply concepts by working on the suggested projects (and your own ideas!)**
  • Explore related concepts within a level concurrently for a holistic understanding.
  • Don't hesitate to revisit earlier topics if you encounter challenges with advanced material.
  • Focus on understanding *why* things work, not just *how* to use them.
  • For projects, start simple and add complexity. Document your design choices and challenges.
